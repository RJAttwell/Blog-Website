Node Modules used:
- Lodash
- Body Parser
- Express
- EJS

On the home page, the compose button takes you to the compose page where you can enter a title and any content you wish in the content text area. After publish
is pressed, it'll take you back to the home page where your post has been logged. The content text is limited to 100 characters using the substring method.

There is an anchor tag that says "Read More". When clicked, it'll take you to a dedicated page for your post. 

On the contact page, there is a form that'll send me an email as well as my professional socials.
